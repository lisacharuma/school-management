The TechSisters Coding School

At TechSisters School, we are committed to providing exceptional education and fostering a supportive learning environment for all students. With a rich history of academic excellence and a dedication to nurturing individual talents, we aim to inspire lifelong learners and responsible citizens.

Our Mission

Our mission at TechSisters School is to empower students to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally. We strive to cultivate critical thinking skills, creativity, and a passion for learning in every student, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in an ever-changing world.

History

Established in 2024 from a place where access to a computer was a priviledge to a very few, TechSisters School commits to providing a high-quality tech education that prepares students for future success.

Principal's Message

As the principal of TechSisters School, I am proud to lead a dedicated team of educators who are passionate about student success in the tech world. Together, we work tirelessly to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment where every student feels valued and empowered to achieve their goals, with me being the example to show that nothing is impossible as long as you commit to it.

Faculty and Staff

Our team of experienced and caring faculty members are dedicated to providing personalized attention and support to each student. With expertise in their respective fields and a genuine commitment to student success, our faculty members go above and beyond to ensure that every student reaches their full potential.

Academic Programs

At TechSisters School, our main focus is to pave way for everyone to fit in the evergrowing digital world. In that regard, we offer academic programs designed to meet the unique needs and interests of our students. From rigorous college-preparatory courses to specialized programs in STEM, we provide opportunities for students to explore their passions and pursue their dreams.

Student Life

Beyond the classroom, TechSisters School offers a vibrant and engaging student life experience. From student clubs and organizations to community service projects and cultural events, there are countless opportunities for students to get involved, make connections, and create lasting memories.

Community Engagement

TechSisters School is proud to be an active member of the local community. Through partnerships with businesses, organizations, and community leaders, we strive to create meaningful opportunities for students to engage with the world around them and make a positive impact.
